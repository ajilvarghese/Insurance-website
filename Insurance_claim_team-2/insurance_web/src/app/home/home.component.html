What does your health insurance policy give you?

Daycare expenses

Treatments that take less than 24 hours are known as daycare treatments. Your health insurance policy My Health Insurance offers financial aid for expenses incurred towards these daycare treatments.
Hospitalization

The plan covers surgeries, doctor consultation fees, room rent, ICU expenses due to injury or illness, ambulances, and many more facilities. You may even avail of a cashless treatment if you are admitted to a network facility and meet the other authorization criteria for a claim.
Extended hospitalization

My Health Insurance insurance policies grant allowance for extended hospitalization and child hospitalization under the extended hospitalization benefits of your My Health Insurance cover.
Pre hospitalization expenses

Cost of therapies and consultation fees for the period of 30 days, which can also extend up to 60 days, before hospitalization due to the same injury or illness.
Post hospitalization expenses

Cost of therapies and consultation fees for the period of 60 days, which can also extend up to 90 days, after hospitalization due to the same injury or illness.
Other treatment

Expenses incurred towards ayurved, Unani, Siddha, or homoeopathic treatments from a recognized institute or under an authorized doctor are included in your health insurance policy. These can include surgical and therapeutic procedures.
Organ donor expenses

Health insurance covers the whole cost of organ donation and transplant surgeries.
Recovery benefit

Financial support if you stay at the hospital longer than you expected, known as recovery benefit, that can help you and your family meet miscellaneous expenses while you recover, are included in your health insurance cover.
